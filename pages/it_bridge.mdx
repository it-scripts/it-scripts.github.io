Bridge resource that makes it possible for all future it-scripts to be compatible with the following frameworks, inventories, etc:  

### Supported Frameworks
- `✅` **| ESX**
- `✅` **| QbCore**
- `✅` **| QBox**
- `✅` **| NDCore**

### Supported Inventorys
- `✅` **| esx_inventory**
- `✅` **| qb_inventory**
- `✅` **| ps-inventory**
- `✅` **| qs_inventory**
- `✅` **| ox_inventory**
- `✅` **| codem-inventory**
- `✅` **| origen-inventory**

### Supported Interactions
- `✅` **| ox_target**
- `✅` **| qb-target**
- `✅` **| distance interaction (press [E])**

### Supported Notifications
- `✅` **| brutal_notify**
- `✅` **| ox_lib**
- `✅` **| esx_notify**
- `✅` **| qb-core**
- `✅` **| es_extended**
- `✅` **| mythic_notify**
- `✅` **| okokNotify**

### Supported TextUIs
- `✅` **| ox_lib**
- `✅` **| qb-core**
- `✅` **| esx_textui**

### Supported Menus
- `✅` **| esx_context**
- `✅` **| ox_lib - context**
- `✅` **| qb-menu**

### Supported Menus
- `✅` **| qs-dispatch**
- `✅` **| ps-dispatch**
- `✅` **| cd_disaptch**
- `✅` **| codem_dispatch**
- `✅` **| emergency_dispatch**
- `✅` **| origen_police**
